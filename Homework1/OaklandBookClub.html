About Us

We are a group of diverse individuals who all share one common trait - the love of a good book! If you also love good books, why don't you consider joining our group? Some of the benefits to joining are:

  • You get to read lots of great books!
  • You get to meet new and interesting people
  • You get to visit lots of different locations in beautiful Oakland!
